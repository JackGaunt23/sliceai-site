How it works

Three steps to a better swing

1

Import your swing

Record or pick a single down-the-line swing video from your library and trim it to the moment that matters.

2

Cloud AI analysis

Your swing is uploaded securely and analyzed in the cloud. SliceAI detects the key positions from Address through Finish and runs fundamentals checks at each one.

3

See your score

Get a SliceAI Score out of 10, then explore an annotated, position-by-position breakdown to see exactly where your swing breaks down.

SliceAI Score

A 0–10 grade for improving golfers. The SliceAI Score grades swing fundamentals to guide your practice. It is coaching guidance, not a tour-level rating or a substitute for a professional instructor.

Honest about how it works

SliceAI analysis runs in the cloud. Your swing video is uploaded for processing and nothing is analyzed on your device. The first analysis can take a couple of minutes while the model warms up.
